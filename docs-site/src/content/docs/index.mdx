## What is Dispatcher?

Dispatcher is a Chrome extension that sits on top of OneSchool and automates the most repetitive administrative workflows — Supervision scheduling, Relief allocation, and clash detection — using a local AI that runs on your school's own server.

**No data leaves your school network.** The AI runs on-premises (not in the cloud), and student and staff identifiers are tokenised before leaving the browser.

## Key features

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Supervision scheduling" icon="pencil">
    Automatically propose supervision allocations based on current staff availability and timetable data.

  <Card title="Relief allocation" icon="random">
    Find available relief teachers and generate relief plans without manual cross-referencing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Clash detection" icon="warning">
