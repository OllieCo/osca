## Getting help

**Documentation first:** Most questions are answered in this documentation. Use the search bar (top of the page) or browse the sidebar.

**Email support:** support@dispatcher.app  
Response time: within 1 business day for Tier 1 plans; within 4 business hours for Tier 2 plans.

**Security issues:** security@dispatcher.app (not the general support address — handled separately).

## Reporting a bug

1. Check the [FAQ](/reference/faq/) to see if it's a known issue.
2. Collect the following:
   - Chrome version (`chrome://version`)
   - Dispatcher extension version (visible in `chrome://extensions`)
   - Server health output: `curl http://<server-ip>:3001/api/health`
   - Error message or screenshot from the extension side panel
3. Email the above to support@dispatcher.app with the subject line `Bug: [brief description]`.

## Feature requests

Email support@dispatcher.app with the subject line `Feature request: [brief description]`. We read every request — popular requests are added to the public roadmap.

## Service status

Check [dispatcher.app/status](https://dispatcher.app/status) for current service availability and incident history.

*Note: The status page covers the Dispatcher licensing and billing service only. Your AI inference runs on your own server — its availability is independent of ours.*
